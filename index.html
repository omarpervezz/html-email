Invitation to Participate in Research on Challenges and Opportunities for CSOs and NGOs in Zambia

Dear Colleagues,

Greetings. I am Darren Watson, a doctoral candidate in International Politics at Manchester Metropolitan University in the UK. My research focuses on "The Challenges and Opportunities for CSOs and NGOs in the post-SDGs era," specifically focusing on Zambian organisations. My previous experience of living in Zambia and my respect and love for the country have fuelled my passion for this research.

I am reaching out to request your organisation's participation in essential research as we navigate the complexities of a rapidly evolving Zambia, characterised by climate change, load shedding, donor fatigue, shrinking aid budgets, and shifting global priorities. I believe your input will be crucial in identifying key trends, emerging issues, and innovative solutions to bolster the sector's resilience and impact.

As we approach the conclusion of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) era, it is vital to comprehend the experiences, challenges, and aspirations of CSOs and NGOs in Zambia. Your organisation's unique insights are invaluable to this study. Your participation will contribute to a broader understanding of the factors influencing the sustainability of CSOs and NGOs in Zambia and help shape policy decisions and resource allocation. Your participation is voluntary and confidential.
